The COMPAS (Correctional Offender Management Profiling for Alternative Sanctions) dataset is used to predict the likelihood of a defendant re-offending within two years.

Attributes:

Attribute Description
sex The sex of the defendant (e.g., Male, Female).
age The age of the defendant at the time of the crime.
race The race of the defendant (e.g., Caucasian, African-American).
juv_fel_count The number of juvenile felony charges the defendant has.
juv_misd_count The number of juvenile misdemeanor charges the defendant has.
juv_other_count The number of other juvenile charges the defendant has.
priors_count The number of prior criminal charges the defendant has.
c_charge_degree The degree of the current charge (e.g., Misdemeanor, Felony).
two_year_recid A binary variable indicating whether the defendant recidivated (re-offended) within two years (1: Yes, 0: No).

Goal:

The goal of analyzing the COMPAS dataset is to predict the likelihood of a defendant re-offending within two years based on their demographic information and criminal history.

There will be 10 classification tasks following, where the time taken for each task is important. Please proceed efficiently.
