Thank you for participating in our research survey! This study is part of a thesis project focused on Explainable AI, specifically exploring decision trees.

In this survey, you will be represented with 4 different datasets.

For each dataset you will be provided by a description and a decision tree will be displayed.

Your task is to classify 10 samples of the dataset based on the decision tree displayed.

Only during the classification tasks, we will measure the time taken for each classification. Please proceed efficiently and avoid unnecessary delays.

Please take as much time as you need to read the descriptions of the datasets.

After that you will be asked to measure the confidence of your answers, the trust you have in the model and provide a small description of the model.

Feel free to add any comments you want!

Your participation in this survey is entirely voluntary. You are free to stop at any time without any consequences. There is no reward or profit for participating in this survey.

Your responses will greatly contribute to our research and understanding of Explainable AI.
